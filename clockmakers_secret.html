The Clockmaker's Secret

Room Description

Tucked away in a quiet Alpine town stands a forgotten workshop, its tick-tock heartbeat long silenced. You’ve received a cryptic letter from the late Horatio Bellamy, a reclusive horologist whose timepieces were rumored to do far more than mark the hour. His final invention — a clock said to conceal a secret capable of altering the course of history — was never found. Inside, brass gears and velvet-lined drawers whisper of precision, legacy, and danger. But the mechanism is already in motion, and the hands are turning. Can you uncover what Horatio hid before time runs out?

Room Details

  • Difficulty: 5/5
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Capacity: 2-6 players
  • Recommended Age: 16+

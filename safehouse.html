Safehouse

Room Description

The year is 1963. In a nondescript apartment tucked away behind the Iron Curtain, you've been summoned by an old acquaintance — a double agent who's gone silent. What was once a covert drop point and safehouse is now eerily abandoned, save for a coded message and a ticking countdown to extraction.

As the Gestapo close in and you hurry to discover the reason behind your agent's disappearance, secrets buried in old dossiers, scrambled radio transmissions, and Cold War paranoia begin coming to light. Is this a trap? A test? Or the last chance to stop something far worse?

Your mission: uncover your contact's fate, decipher their final message, and escape unseen before the safehouse is raided and your window closes — for good.

Room Details

  • Difficulty: 3/5
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Capacity: 2-4 players
